About Me

I've always had a deep love for two things: learning and technology. My dream was to become a librarian, and I received my Masters in Library and Information Science to pursue that goal. During my program however, I found a desire to learn more about the systems behind the library. Due to that, I took every course offered to me relating to technology.

After years in the workforce working with SQL, I made the decision to take a full-time, immersive software developer bootcamp at Eleven-Fifty Academy. I aimed to quench the yearning I had towards software development. After graduating, I am now ready to begin my dream of becoming a software developer.
